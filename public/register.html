Why CGCM Map

CGCM Map is a reliable and low cost medium for the members of the Church of God in Christ Mennonite to find their way around the Conference. How CGCM Map is designed is, one person from every congregation in the conference is required to add and maintain the locations in their congregation. We have design CGCM Map in such a way that this job will be quick and painless. If we all do our part, CGCM Map will be the helpful and dependable tool we envision it to be.

Adding your congregation

To add your congregation to CGCM Map you will need to apply for an account, be accepted and buy the necessary location credits. Once accepted you will receive an email with more instructions and a password. Once you sign in with your email and password, an option in the navigation drawer will appear called 'Congregational Agent'. This is where you can add edit and delete locations.

Location credits

We charge a smal one time fee to help offset some of the development cost. The cost for one location credit is 5$. You can buy them in packages of 5, 15, 25, 50 or 75. If you have more than 75 households in your congregation that you would like to be on the map, just buy as many packages you need until everyone is added. You do get 3 free location credits when you sign up to give you an idea of how CGCM Map works.
